Making Pictogram Construction Visible: Designing Generative Tools for Professional AAC Practice

Three parallel lines of work — a literature review, empirical engagement with AAC professionals, and iterative development of a proof-of-concept system — have shaped each other throughout the process.
All three converge on PICTOS.net, a three-stage pictogram generator whose modular architecture is workshop-ready for co-design sessions.
Herbert Spencer González — PhD Candidate in Design, Auckland University of Technology
